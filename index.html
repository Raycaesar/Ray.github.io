Tutorial:
  • Each player announces a card in turn.
  • If a player announces a card, red or black, and another following player only believes the opposite color of that card, the follower unfollows the announcer.
  • A player believes a color of a card if it holds a card of the same color, or announced a card of the same color or received a card of the same color from a player he is following.
  • If a player announces a card and another non-following player has the same color of that card, the non-follower starts following the announcer.
  • Players who lose all followers are defeated.
  • The last remaining player wins the game.
